Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 1 tbsp
Baking Powder 3 1/2 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Flour 1 1/2 cups
Milk 1 1/4 cup
Eggs 1
Melted Butter 3 tbsp

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Step 2: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Apple Pie

  • Time: 1 hr 30 min
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
White Sugar 1/2 cup
Brown Sugarr 1/2 cup
Pastry 9 inch recipe
Flour 3 tbsp
Butter 1/2 cup
Water 1/4 cup
Apples: Peeled, Cored,Sliced 8

Step 1: Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste. Add water, white sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.

Step 2: Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it does not run off.

Step 3: Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.

Pecan Pie

  • Time: 1hr 10 min
  • Serves: 10 people
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 1 3/4 cups
Dar corn syrup 1/4 cup
Butter 1/4 cup
Cold water 1 tbsp
Cornstarch 2 tsp
Eggs 3
Salt 1/4 tsp
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Chopped Pecans 1 1/4 cup
Pie shell 1 (9 inch)

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2: In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar, corn syrup, butter, water, and cornstarch. Bring to a full boil, and remove from heat.

Step 3: In a large bowl, beat eggs until frothy. Gradually beat in cooked syrup mixture. Stir in salt, vanilla, and pecans. Pour into pie shell.

Step 4: Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until filling is set.
